Seasonal Crops

Summer
In this time of the year,days become Warm,
Hot and really long, while nights in this season
are the shortest.
So the crops in summers are listed as :

1. Peanut

2. Sugarcane

3. Corn
   
Winter
It is the season with the shortest days and
the lowest temperatures.
So the crops in winter are listed as :

1. Maize

2. Rice

3. Bajra




Rainy
The Rainy season is the time of the year when most
of the region's average annual rainfall occurs.
So the crops in rainy are listed as :

1. Capsicum

2. Onion

3. Spinach

   
Spring
In this season, days become longer and
weather gets warmer in the temperate zone .
So the crops in are listed as :

1. Beetroot

2. Soya Bean

3. Wheat
